About Us

BlaBlaBoats is a website intended to connect boat owners and passionate people, who just want to experience more! It can be used for lots of different purposes. BlaBlaBoats is primarily used by two groups of people, those who publish trips (boat owners) and those, who are looking for a nice ride (hitchhikers):

Boat owners, who...
  • have already planned a trip, but are willing to take hitchhikers with them.
  • have a regatta, and are still looking for crew members.
  • organize expeditions and want to sell tickets for it.
Hitchhikers, who...
  • need to get from a place A to a place B, and want to do it in a fun and eco friendly way.
  • want to try sailing, but aren't sure if it's for them.
  • want to gain more experience before buying a boat for themselves.
  • want to meet new interesting people.

Getting started

  1. Create your account. To be able to use all the functionalities of BlaBlaBoats, you need to create an account. Don't worry, it is completely free! First you need to click on the Sign Up link on the menu bar. Then fill out the form and submit it. You will then be asked to verify your email-address. Do it and then you're ready to go!
  2. Your dashboard. After creating your account, you will be redirected to your own dashboard. On that page you can see your personal information and all the trips you have created. To access this page at any given time, click on your username in the menu bar *, and then click on Dashboard.
  3. Personalize your account. For other people to better understand who you might be, you need to personalize your account. In order to do so, click on your username in the menu bar *, and then click on Settings. Once on the Settings-page, you can add a user description and upload a profile picture. You can also change your username, first name and last name.
  4. Search for trips. To search for trips you can type in keywords in the search box in the menu bar and then hit Enter. However there is also an extended search function. Go to the home page and click on the Show Filters button. This will display all extended search features:
    • The first filter allows you to show only trips that are in a given range of a location set by you.
    • The second filter permits you to only show the trips that are within a given time-span set by you.
    • The third filter allows you to only search for specific types of boats, sailing modes and how many people could come.
    • In the last row you can set how you want the results to be sorted and how many results you want per page.
    Please note, that you can combine different filters (i.e. location and time span...) and you can remove the filters by clicking the Reset button on the right.
  5. Create your own trip. To create your own trip go to your Dashboard page, switch to the Planned Trips tab and click the Create a new Trip button. Then, you need to set a title and write a description. In the destination section, you can set all your stopovers. This is optional but strongly suggested. Then you can add more info in the More Information section. Once all this is done, click the Create Trip button to save your trip. Note, that you can still edit some details later. Only after having created your trip, you will be able to add images to it.
* Please note that if you don't see your username on the menu bar, you need to Log In to your account.
